Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information and data in the Water Tracker app (hereinafter referred to as "the App").

Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information:

  • Water intake data
  • App settings
  • Daily intake goals

All this information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Recording and tracking your water intake
  • Providing and improving app functionality
  • Enhancing user experience

Data Protection

We prioritize your privacy in the App. We protect your data through the following measures:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No automatic transmission to external servers
  • Use of encrypted storage

Third-Party Data Sharing

The App does not share any collected data with third parties.

Data Deletion

Users can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the App from their device.

Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. We will notify users through the App when significant changes are made.
